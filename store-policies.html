Store Policies

These policies explain who we are, what information we collect, how manual payment verification works, and what customers can expect after ordering.

Store Identity

Unlimited Topup is an independent digital game store operated online. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or an official reseller of the game publishers, Google, Microsoft, Xbox, Rockstar Games, Garena, Krafton, Riot Games, or Mojang. Product names, logos, and trademarks belong to their respective owners.

Privacy

We collect only the information needed to create and verify an order, such as account email, player details, selected product, payment reference, and payment screenshot. We never request your Google password, game password, bank password, card PIN, UPI PIN, or OTP. Do not submit those details anywhere on this website.

Payments

Payments are made manually using the UPI information shown during checkout. A payment screenshot and a valid transaction reference are requested only to match the payment with the order. Submitting an order does not mean payment has been approved; every payment is reviewed first.

Delivery

Topups and activation keys are processed after payment verification. Delivery times may vary depending on payment confirmation, product availability, and the accuracy of the customer information supplied. Order status is available through Your Orders.

Refunds

Contact us before delivery if you believe a payment was duplicated or an order contains an error. Completed topups, revealed activation keys, and delivered redeem codes normally cannot be cancelled or refunded. If we cannot fulfil a paid order, we will arrange a correction or refund after verification.

Terms

You must provide accurate order and payment information and use a payment method you are authorised to use. Fraudulent, altered, duplicated, or unrelated payment proof may result in rejection. Product availability and prices may change before an order is submitted.

Contact

For order or policy questions, email unlimitedtopup001@gmail.com. Include your order reference, but never send a password, OTP, UPI PIN, or banking credential.
